Retrieval Augmented Generation for Knowledge Intensive NLP Tasks
Authors: Lewis, Perez, Piktus, Petroni, Karpukhin et al
Institution: Facebook AI Research, 2020
RAG combines parametric memory (LLM weights) with non-parametric memory (retrieved documents)
Two main components: Dense Passage Retriever and BART sequence-to-sequence generator
Retriever uses bi-encoder to encode queries and documents into dense vector space
Top-k documents retrieved via maximum inner product search
Generator conditions on retrieved documents plus query to produce answer
Outperforms pure parametric models by 4.3 BLEU points on open domain QA
Allows model knowledge to be updated without retraining
Hallucination reduced because answers are grounded in retrieved text
Foundation for modern enterprise AI systems using ChromaDB, Pinecone, Weaviate
