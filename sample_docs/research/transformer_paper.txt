Attention Is All You Need - Research Summary
Authors: Vaswani, Shazeer, Parmar, Uszkoreit, Jones, Gomez, Kaiser, Polosukhin
Institution: Google Brain and Google Research, 2017
The Transformer architecture relies entirely on attention mechanisms
Eliminates recurrent neural networks and convolutions entirely
Multi-head attention allows model to jointly attend to information from different subspaces
Encoder: stack of 6 identical layers each with multi-head attention and feed-forward sublayers
Decoder: stack of 6 identical layers with additional cross-attention over encoder output
Positional encoding added to input embeddings to retain sequence order
Base model: 65M parameters, Big model: 213M parameters
Training time: 3.5 days on 8 NVIDIA P100 GPUs
BLEU score: 28.4 on English-German translation
This paper became the foundation for BERT, GPT, and all modern large language models
