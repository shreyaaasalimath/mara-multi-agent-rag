Standard Employment Contract Key Clauses
At-Will Employment: Either party may terminate at any time with or without cause
Non-Disclosure Agreement: Employee agrees not to disclose confidential company information
Non-Compete: Employee agrees not to work for direct competitors for 12 months post-termination
Intellectual Property: All work created during employment belongs exclusively to employer
Arbitration: All disputes resolved through binding arbitration, waiving right to jury trial
Severance: Employee entitled to 2 weeks base pay per year of service, maximum 26 weeks
Benefits: Health insurance, 401k with 4% employer match, 15 days PTO
Probationary Period: First 90 days probationary, termination requires no severance
Governing Law: Agreement governed by laws of the State of California
Notice Period: Either party must provide 2 weeks written notice before resignation
