GDPR General Data Protection Regulation Complete Reference
Effective Date: May 25, 2018
Jurisdiction: Applies to all organizations processing EU citizens data regardless of location
Six Lawful Bases: Consent, Contract, Legal obligation, Vital interests, Public task, Legitimate interests
Consent Requirements: Must be freely given, specific, informed, and unambiguous
Data Subject Rights: Access, Rectification, Erasure, Portability, Objection
Data Breach Notification: Must notify supervisory authority within 72 hours
Data Protection Officer: Mandatory for large-scale systematic monitoring or sensitive data
Tier 1 Penalties: Up to 10 million euros or 2% of global annual turnover
Tier 2 Penalties: Up to 20 million euros or 4% of global annual turnover
Privacy by Design: Data protection must be built into systems from the outset
Cross-Border Transfers: Require adequacy decision or standard contractual clauses
